## App Description (4000 characters max)

SnapOCR is a powerful screenshot OCR tool that instantly extracts text from any area of your screen. Simply select a region, and the app recognizes the text and copies it to your clipboard.

**Key Features:**

• **Instant Text Recognition** - Select any region on your screen and get the text in seconds
• **Multi-Language Support** - Recognizes English and Chinese (Simplified) text out of the box
• **Math Formula Support** - Automatically detects and converts mathematical formulas to LaTeX format
• **One-Click Copy** - Extracted text is automatically copied to your clipboard
• **Privacy First** - All processing happens locally on your device. No internet required. No data collected.
• **Self-Contained** - No external dependencies to install. Everything is bundled in the app.

**How to Use:**

1. Launch SnapOCR
2. Select a region on your screen by dragging your mouse
3. Release to capture and perform OCR
4. The recognized text is automatically copied to your clipboard
5. Paste anywhere with Cmd+V

**Perfect for:**

- Extracting text from images and screenshots
- Copying text from PDFs that don't allow selection
- Recognizing mathematical formulas
- Quick data entry from visual sources
- Translating text from images

**Keyboard Shortcut Setup:**

Use macOS built-in Shortcuts app or Automator to create a keyboard shortcut:
1. Open Shortcuts app
2. Create a new shortcut to open SnapOCR
3. Add your preferred keyboard shortcut (e.g., Cmd+Shift+O)

**Privacy:**

SnapOCR operates entirely offline. All OCR processing happens on your Mac. We don't collect, store, or transmit any data.
