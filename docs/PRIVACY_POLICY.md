# Privacy Policy for SnapOCR

**Last Updated: February 2025**

## Introduction

SnapOCR ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use it.

## Information We Collect

**SnapOCR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically, we do NOT collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Device identifiers
- Screenshots or images you capture
- OCR text results

## How the App Works

SnapOCR operates entirely on your device:
1. You select a region of your screen to capture
2. The app processes the image locally using Tesseract OCR
3. The extracted text is copied to your clipboard
4. No data ever leaves your device

## Third-Party Services

SnapOCR uses the following technologies that run entirely on your device:
- **Tesseract OCR** - Open-source optical character recognition engine
- **RapidLatexOCR** - For mathematical formula recognition

Neither of these technologies transmit any data externally.

## Permissions

SnapOCR requests the following permissions:
- **Screen Recording** - To capture screenshots of selected regions
- **Clipboard Access** - To copy extracted text to your clipboard

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and no data is transmitted externally.

## Children's Privacy

SnapOCR does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, our app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
